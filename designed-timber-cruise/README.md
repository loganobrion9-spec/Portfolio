# Timber Cruise Design and Mapping

## Project Overview
This project was completed as a final for FORS 202 Forest Mensuration at the University of Montana. It consisted of delineating a forest stand, designing a cruise, mapping baselines and cruiselines, and then exporting the map to ArcGIS Field Maps for use on the ground.

## Objective
To design and execute a timber cruise of a stand in Pattee Canyon, Missoula, Montana

## Data Sources
All data for this project, including the forest stand boundaries and cruise design, were created by me in ArcGIS Pro.
